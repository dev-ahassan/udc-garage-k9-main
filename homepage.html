Welcome to myUDC Garage Self-Service Portal...

Through this portal students and school officials can perform the following tasks:


  • Students have the ability to report any witnessed garage related incidents anonymously.

  • Students have the ability to purchase Parking Passes from the comofort of their mobile devices.

  • School officials have the ability to look up vehicles using their license plate numbers, and contact their owners immediately during case of emergencies.
